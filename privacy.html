Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

1. Scope

ytautott is a personal, single-operator content studio. It does not offer public accounts and does not collect data from the general public. This policy describes the data the Service handles for the accounts its Operator connects.

2. Data accessed

When the Operator connects a TikTok account through TikTok's official APIs, the Service may access, under the scopes the Operator grants:

  • Basic profile information (display name, avatar, open id)
  • Profile and account statistics (e.g. follower, likes, and video counts)
  • The list of the account's public videos and their view metrics
  • The ability to upload and publish videos to that account

3. How data is used

Data is used solely to (a) publish the Operator's own original videos to the Operator's own accounts and (b) track how that content performs so the Operator can improve it. It is not used for advertising, profiling of third parties, or any other purpose.

4. Sharing and selling

The Service does not sell, rent, or share accessed data with third parties. Data flows only between the Service, the connected platform (e.g. TikTok), and the infrastructure used to operate the integration.

5. Storage and retention

Performance metrics are stored in the Operator's private records for as long as they are useful for analyzing content performance. Access tokens are stored securely and can be revoked at any time.

6. Revoking access

Access granted to the Service can be revoked at any time from the connected platform's settings (for TikTok: Settings → Security → Apps) or by contacting the Operator.
